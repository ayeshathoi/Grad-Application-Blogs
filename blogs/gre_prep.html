Short Suggestion

I am going to give a very very short suggestion here. If you are determined to take GRE, then you should:

  • Register for GRE at first (keep 2-4 months at best). In this way, you will feel a push to study.
  • Solve PPP1 from ETS to judge your initial state
  • Follow GREGMAT 2 months plan (It also has toefl study plan of 14 days. You can be benefitted from this). One month subscription fee is $8.
  • Materials: GRE Prep Material (Boooks and Others)
  • Follow GREGMAT Vocabulary and Use QUIZLET
  • Solve PPP2 from ETS to judge your end state
